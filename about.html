Our Story

TollPay was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: to eliminate the frustration of toll plaza congestion and create a seamless payment experience for drivers.

What started as a small team of transportation enthusiasts has grown into a leading provider of toll management solutions, serving millions of drivers and hundreds of toll plazas across the country.

Our innovative approach combines cutting-edge technology with user-friendly interfaces to create a system that benefits both drivers and toll operators.
