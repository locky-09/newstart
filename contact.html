Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for TollPay?

You can register for TollPay by clicking the "Get Started" button on our homepage and following the registration process. You'll need to provide your vehicle information and payment details to complete the setup.

How does the automated toll collection work?

Our system uses RFID technology to identify your vehicle as it approaches a toll plaza. The toll amount is automatically deducted from your TollPay account, allowing you to drive through without stopping. You'll receive a notification confirming the transaction.

Can I manage multiple vehicles under one account?

Yes, you can add multiple vehicles to your TollPay account. Our Premium and Business plans allow for multiple vehicle management, with the Business plan supporting unlimited vehicles.

What happens if I don't have enough funds in my account?

If your account balance is insufficient, we'll send you a notification. Depending on your plan, we may process the payment using your backup payment method. You can also set up auto-recharge to ensure your account always has sufficient funds.
